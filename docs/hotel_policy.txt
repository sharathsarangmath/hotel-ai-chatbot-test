The Grand Sarang Hotel - Policy Document

CHECK-IN AND CHECK-OUT
Check-in time is 3:00 PM.
Check-out time is 11:00 AM.
Early check-in is subject to availability and may incur additional charges.
Late check-out is subject to availability and must be requested at the front desk.

PARKING
Parking is complimentary for all guests.
Valet parking is available at $15 per night.
Electric vehicle charging stations are available in the parking garage.
Parking is available on a first come first served basis.

PETS
Pets are not allowed on the premises.
Service animals are welcome and exempt from the pet policy.
Guests found with unauthorised pets will be charged a $200 cleaning fee.

DINING
The restaurant serves breakfast from 7:00 AM to 11:00 AM.
Lunch is served from 12:00 PM to 3:00 PM.
Dinner is served from 6:00 PM to 10:00 PM.
Room service is available 24 hours a day.
The rooftop bar is open from 5:00 PM to midnight.

FACILITIES
The hotel has a rooftop pool open from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
The gym is open 24 hours a day.
The spa is open from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM.
Spa treatments must be booked at least 24 hours in advance.
The business centre is open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

ROOMS
The hotel has 200 rooms across 15 floors.
Room types include Standard, Deluxe, Suite, and Presidential Suite.
All rooms include complimentary Wi-Fi.
Non-smoking policy applies to all rooms and public areas.

CANCELLATION POLICY
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in.
Cancellations within 48 hours will be charged one night's stay.
No-shows will be charged the full booking amount.

LOCAL AREA
The hotel is located in the city centre.
The nearest airport is 30 minutes away by taxi.
The nearest metro station is a 5 minute walk from the hotel.
